Data Privacy Act (Republic Act No. 10173) Compliance

Our Municipal Health Information System is fully committed to protecting the privacy and security of all personal and sensitive personal information collected, processed, and stored within its operations, in strict compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

We ensure that:

  • Information is collected and used only for legitimate purposes related to public health services.
  • Data is stored securely with appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
  • Individuals have the right to access, correct, and object to the processing of their personal data, subject to legal limitations.
  • Personnel are trained on data privacy principles and responsible data handling practices.
  • Consent is obtained where necessary for data collection and processing.

By registering and using this system, you acknowledge and agree to our adherence to these data privacy principles. For a complete understanding of your rights and our obligations, please refer to our full Data Privacy Policy.
