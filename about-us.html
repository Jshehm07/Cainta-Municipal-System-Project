About Us

The Municipal Health Info System is a community-centered digital platform created to strengthen local healthcare delivery and management. We aim to transform traditional health service processes into streamlined, paperless, and efficient systems that benefit both the medical staff and the public.

Our system was built with accessibility, accuracy, and data security at its core. By offering a centralized platform for health-related services, we empower barangay health centers and municipal health offices to monitor, record, and respond to public health needs more efficiently.

Our mission is to:

  • Provide a user-friendly digital experience for patients and health workers
  • Enhance coordination between health personnel across departments
  • Ensure accurate and real-time access to health records and reports
  • Support data-driven decisions through organized health monitoring tools
